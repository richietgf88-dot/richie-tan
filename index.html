The Journey

The Journey
by Richie Tan

Life's journey encompasses the awe-inspiring stages of infancy, childhood's wonder, the transformative quest of adolescence, the independence of young adulthood, the responsibilities of the middle years, the wisdom of aging, and the legacy we leave behind.

See the Own Collection
The Philosophy

My art exists to show that pressure, rejection, and being alone don't break you — they build you.

Every piece reflects transformation and the strength forged through life's challenges. Some works explore texture and colour; others incorporate materials like crystal to highlight resilience — each a reflection of survival and brilliance.

Born in Malaysia in 2001 and entirely self-taught, Richie paints with the whole body — splashes, strokes and layered compositions in the lineage of Jackson Pollock.
